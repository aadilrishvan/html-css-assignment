About College

NMSM Government College is a Government college in Wayanad. It offers UG and PG courses in different subjects.

The college has a library, computer lab, sports facilities and experienced teachers.

Vision

To provide quality education and build responsible citizens.

Mission

To encourage knowledge, discipline and innovation among students.

Facilities

  • Library
  • Computer Lab
  • Sports Ground
  • Wi-Fi Campus
