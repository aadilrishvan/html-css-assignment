Welcome

Welcome to NMSM Government College. NMSM (Neelikkandy Moideen Sahib Memorial) Government College, Kalpetta, is one of the premier higher education institutions in Wayanad district, Kerala. Established in 1981 under the Government of Kerala, the college was founded with the aim of providin quality higher education to students, particularly those from rural, tribal, and economically disadvantaged backgrounds. The college is situated at Vellaramkunnu, near Kalpetta, on a scenic campus surrounded by the natural beauty of Wayanad. Wayanad, empowering students through quality education, skill development, and community engagement while fostering values of social responsibility and lifelong learning.
